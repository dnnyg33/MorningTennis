FAQ

What if someone scheduled can't make it or doesn't show?

If one of the 4 can no longer come, please contact one of the alternates to play. You can find phone numbers by hovering over or clicking on names

What if i don't see my name after completing the form?

Sometimes the schedule doesn't update right away, but as long as you complete the form your info and spot will be saved. If after an hour, it still doesn't update, let me know and I will fix it.

Why are there numbers next to everyone's name?

The number next to a name indicates the preference for that choice. Ideally everyone is playing on their most preferred days so all players would have 1's and 2's, but it depends on how many people sign up and if there is variety on which days are preferred.

When does the schedule "lock"?

The cutoff for submitting your playing days is 6pm on the Sunday night before. This way the players on Monday can go to bed without worrying about getting bumped off the schedule.

How does it decide when people play?

Let's say Bob and Alice are the only ones to submit. Bob requested Wednesday, Tuesday then Monday, while Alice requested Monday then Tuesday. Bob would get first priority for Wednesday, followed by Alice for Monday. After everyone's first choice, it gives priority in reverse order. So now Alice would get first pick for Tuesday, then Bob for Tuesday. Finally Bob would then also get Monday. If you've ever played Settlers of Catan, it's the same picking algorithm for placing inital settlements.
